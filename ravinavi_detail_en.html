The Adventure of Ravi 'n' Navi

For Nintendo Switch™ / Steam®



Product Summary

・Platform：Nintendo Switch / Steam


Overview

Action game for Nintendo Switch™ created using Unity.
The main character "Ravi", a rabbit, and his friend "Navi", a fairy, go on an adventure to get back a carrot stolen by bad turtle.
The unique characters seen along the way, the beautiful scenery, and the interactive music were all created by myself.
