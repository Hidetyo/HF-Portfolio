Ravi And Navi (Disk ver.)

For FDS


I developed game cartridges that could be played on game consoles released in the 1980s, giving full consideration to the rights of the various companies.
The game cartridge for retro game consoles "Ravi and Navi", released in 2023, has been ported to magnetic disk consoles.
Because reusing the disks sold at the time would cause trademark issues, the resin part of the disk was created using a 3D printer.
